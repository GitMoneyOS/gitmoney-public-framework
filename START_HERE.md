## Choose Your Route

### I run an AI-enabled agency

Start here when delivery knowledge, prompts, contractor work, approvals, and client-neutral assets are spread across people and tools.

- First question: can another trusted operator prove what is approved and reusable?
- First asset: an approved client-delivery playbook or decision record.
- Diagnostic: AI Work Governance Signal Audit or AI Office Audit.
- Control gain: less founder dependency and clearer ownership of reusable delivery knowledge.

### I lead a SaaS team using coding agents

Start here when agents produce code or product changes faster than the team can review decisions, architecture, permissions, and evidence.

- First question: can the founder explain what agents may change and how a change becomes official?
- First asset: an approved `AGENTS.md`, product decision, or governed requirement linked to a pull request.
- Diagnostic: Agentic Repo Governance Check.
- Control gain: faster execution with visible human authority and review.

### I own a knowledge business or private vault

Start here when valuable doctrine exists in Obsidian, notes, drives, or your head but does not reliably become offers, operating systems, or approved public assets.

- First question: what should stay private, and what deserves promotion into the company record?
- First asset: an approved doctrine card, offer brief, scorecard, or operating procedure.
- Diagnostic: Private Vault To Official Record Audit.
- Control gain: AI utility without surrendering private memory, voice, or ownership.

### I am a nontraditional AI-era builder

GitBuilt is the contribution-proof lane for builders, designers, creatives, and marketers whose judgment is not captured by activity counts alone.

- First question: what reviewable contribution proves how you thought, decided, improved, or shipped?
- First asset: a public-safe issue, pull request, design record, campaign asset, or release note.
- Pilot status: staged behind evidence, consent, security, and owner-approval gates.
- Control gain: a permissioned contribution story instead of a vanity activity count.

GitBuilt is a community and proof initiative. It is not the primary commercial buyer route and is not represented as GitHub-affiliated.
