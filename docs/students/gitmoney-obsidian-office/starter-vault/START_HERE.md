# START HERE

Welcome to your Obsidian Office. Three layers, say them once out loud:

- **Obsidian is private memory.** This vault. Nobody sees it. Safe to be wrong here.
- **Agent platforms are workbenches.** Where briefed jobs become drafts.
- **GitHub is the official record.** Where approved work becomes assets.

Do not customize anything yet. Do the 30-minute win first.

## The 30-Minute Win

### 1. Capture one real idea (5 min)

Not a test note. A real thought from your actual business or life. Create a note in `00-inbox/` using `_templates/daily-capture.md`. One idea, plainly stated.

### 2. Fill the six student-facing fields (5 min)

In the note's frontmatter:

- `owner`: you.
- `source`: where it came from - your head, a call, a video.
- `status`: `seed`.
- `classification`: `private`.
- `summary`: one sentence for your future self.
- `promotion_target`: leave as `none` for now.

The template also includes `created`. Leave it as today's date.

### 3. Add one real link (5 min)

Finish this sentence inside the note: *"This reminds me of..."* — and link it: `[[like this]]`. If nothing exists to link yet, link it to the idea it came from and write why. One honest link beats ten reflex links.

### 4. Mark its maturity (5 min)

Is it a `seed` (raw), a `sprout` (you have touched it twice), or already an `asset-candidate` (could become a business asset)? Most first notes are seeds. That is correct, not small. The full ladder: `seed`, `sprout`, `asset-candidate`, `ready-to-promote`, `promoted`, `archived`.

### 5. Route it (10 min)

Decide what, if anything, this note should become, and set `promotion_target`:

- `none` - it is memory. Most notes. Perfectly fine.
- `agent-brief` - an agent should do a job with this.
- `github-issue` - it is a trackable task for your organization.
- `repo-doc` - it belongs in a repo as a document.
- `decision-log` - it records a decision.
- `content` - it wants to be content.
- `sop` - it is a procedure you will repeat.

Done. You have a governed thought. That is the whole system in miniature - everything else in this vault just scales what you did.
