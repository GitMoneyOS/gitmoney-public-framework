# Week 4: Governor Review Keeps The Office Alive

Recorded: 2026-06-11
Audience: GitMoneyOS students
Layer classification: GitHub record

## Lesson Objective

Review notes, briefs, decisions, and official-record candidates so the AI Office does not decay.

The system only works if work gets a fate: keep, promote, revise, or archive.

## Worked Example

Review item:

```text
Pricing objection SOP draft
Owner: me
Status: ready-to-promote
Evidence: draft SOP, source note, checklist
Decision: promote to official SOP or keep private another cycle
Next review: next monthly Governor Review
```

## Proof Artifact

Create one owner decision and one lifecycle checkpoint for a note, brief, or official-record candidate.

## Promotion Boundary

Nothing becomes an official asset until it has:

- owner,
- approval path,
- evidence,
- reuse path,
- lifecycle.

## Completion Checklist

- [ ] I reviewed one note.
- [ ] I reviewed one agent brief or draft.
- [ ] I named one official-record candidate.
- [ ] I made one owner decision.
- [ ] I assigned a next review date or archived the work.
