# What Is A Founder AI Office?

## Short Answer

A founder AI Office is a control system that separates private memory, AI-assisted work, and the official company record.

It answers seven owner questions:

1. What knowledge matters?
2. Who owns it?
3. Where does it live?
4. What may AI do with it?
5. How does a human approve the result?
6. How does the result become reusable?
7. When does it get reviewed, replaced, or retired?

## The Three Rooms

### Private Notebook

The founder's source memory. It holds unfinished thinking, research, doctrine, notes, and sensitive context under appropriate boundaries.

### Work Desk

The operating layer. Humans and AI agents receive bounded work orders and create reviewable outputs. This layer can propose. It cannot silently declare company truth.

### Company Record

The approved operating record. Repositories, issues, pull requests, commits, owners, and reviews make changes inspectable.

## Why GitHub

GitHub is useful because it already has the basic controls a business record needs: version history, ownership, proposal lanes, approval, automation, access, and evidence. GitMoneyOS translates those controls into owner language.

The point is not to turn every founder into a developer. The point is to stop valuable business knowledge from living only in a person's head, an AI chat, or a contractor handoff.
